# Code Of Conduct

This project follows a professional, respectful collaboration standard.

## Our Standards

Examples of expected behavior:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussion
- Focus feedback on technical content
- Assume good intent and seek clarity before escalation

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, hostile language, or intimidation
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this policy.

Report conduct concerns by opening a [GitHub issue](https://github.com/raylsnetwork/rbft/issues) labeled `conduct`.
All reports are handled confidentially to the extent possible.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and pull
requests that violate this policy.
